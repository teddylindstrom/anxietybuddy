Anxiety buddy

Your online anxiety assistant

When you get an anxiety attack, try to find "three things you can either - hold, touch or count" For example: You can count how many points your keys have, how many coins you have in your wallet or hold firmly around your wrists. By doing this you create a physical sensation that tries to over-power your mental anxiety that is only existing in your mind. Another helpful exercise is to count your breaths. Like this: Breathe in (count 1), breathe out (count 1), Breathe in (count 2), breathe out (count 2) and so on.. Keep doing it till you've reached 10 and start over until you've come down from the worst part.
