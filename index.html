What is an anxiety attack?

An anxiety attack is a sudden feeling of fear of danger or discomfort created in your mind. You can notice a feeling of pressure over your chest, throat or around your upper body, but it's different for everyone. When you notice the anxiety, click on our "exercises" to get some help through it. Remember, you are all right and this will pass. Please visit www.WHO.com to access more about mental health.
